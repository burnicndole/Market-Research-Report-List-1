<p><strong>The AMOLED Display Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMOLED display materials market comprises two primary types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diodes (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED technology uses a matrix of active circuits to control individual pixels, allowing for higher resolutions and faster refresh rates, making it ideal for smartphones and TVs. In contrast, PMOLED features a simpler structure with passive circuits, suitable for smaller displays like wearables. Both types leverage organic compounds for vibrant color and energy efficiency, driving advancements in display technology.</p></p>
