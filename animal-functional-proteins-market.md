<p><p>The Animal Functional Proteins market encompasses three main types: hydrolysates, concentrates, and isolates. Hydrolysates are peptides obtained through protein breakdown, enhancing digestibility and absorption, making them ideal for nutritional supplements. Concentrates contain a higher protein content than regular protein sources, beneficial for various food applications. Isolates are purified proteins with minimal fat and carbohydrates, often used in sports nutrition and dietary products for their high protein density and functional properties. Together, these products cater to diverse dietary needs and food industry applications.</p></p>
