<p><strong>The Asthma Therapeutic Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long-Term Control Medications</li><li>Quick-Relief Medications</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asthma therapeutic drug market comprises two primary categories: long-term control medications and quick-relief medications. Long-term control medications, such as inhaled corticosteroids and leukotriene modifiers, are designed to reduce inflammation and prevent asthma symptoms over time. In contrast, quick-relief medications, like short-acting beta-agonists, provide rapid relief during acute asthma attacks by relaxing airway muscles. Together, these medication types address both the chronic nature of asthma and the need for immediate symptom relief, enhancing overall management of the condition.</p></p>
