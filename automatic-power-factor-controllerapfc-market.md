<p><p>Automatic Power Factor Controllers (APFCs) enhance electrical system efficiency by maintaining an optimal power factor. The market is segmented into Active APFCs and Passive APFCs. Active APFCs use advanced electronics to dynamically adjust reactive power compensation, providing precise control and efficiency under varying loads. Conversely, Passive APFCs utilize fixed capacitor banks to correct power factor, offering a more cost-effective solution for stable loads. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing energy consumption and reducing electricity costs in various industries.</p></p>
