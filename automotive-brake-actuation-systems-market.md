<p><p>The automotive brake actuation systems market encompasses various components that enhance vehicle safety and performance. Brake boosters amplify driver input, ensuring effective braking with minimal effort. Tandem Master Cylinders (TMCs) improve braking reliability by providing dual circuits for hydraulic fluid distribution, ensuring redundancy in case of a failure. Brake fluid reservoirs store the essential hydraulic fluid needed for braking systems, maintaining proper pressure and performance. Together, these components contribute to the overall efficacy and safety of modern braking systems in vehicles.</p></p>
