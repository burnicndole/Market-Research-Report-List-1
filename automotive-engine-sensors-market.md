<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Process Sensors</li><li>Position Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine sensors market is divided into two primary types: process sensors and position sensors. Process sensors monitor critical engine parameters such as temperature, pressure, and fuel composition, ensuring optimal performance and emissions control. In contrast, position sensors detect the location of engine components, such as the crankshaft and camshaft, aiding in accurate timing and efficiency. Both types play vital roles in enhancing engine management systems, improving fuel efficiency, emissions compliance, and overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
