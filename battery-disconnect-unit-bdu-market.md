<p><p>A Battery Disconnect Unit (BDU) is a critical component in managing electrical systems, particularly in electric vehicles and renewable energy applications. The market is divided into two primary types: Distributed BDU and Centralized BDU. Distributed BDU systems are deployed closer to each battery pack, enhancing safety by isolating power at the source and allowing for modular designs. In contrast, Centralized BDU systems consolidate battery disconnect functions in one location, simplifying wiring and control but potentially increasing response times in emergency situations.</p></p>
