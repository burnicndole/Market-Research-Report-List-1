<p><strong>The Beam Cranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-girder Beam Crane</li><li>Double-girder Beam Crane</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beam cranes market is primarily categorized into three types: single-girder beam cranes, double-girder beam cranes, and others. Single-girder beam cranes feature a single beam that supports the hoist, making them ideal for lighter loads and smaller spaces. Double-girder beam cranes consist of two beams, providing greater lifting capacity and stability for heavier loads. The "others" category includes specialized designs such as underslung cranes and cantilever cranes, catering to unique industrial needs and operational environments.</p></p>
