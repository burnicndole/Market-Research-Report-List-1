<p><p>The camera lens filter market encompasses various types designed to enhance image quality and control light. Band Pass Filters allow specific wavelengths to pass, useful for isolating particular colors. Long Pass Filters transmit longer wavelengths while blocking shorter ones, ideal for low-light scenarios. Short Pass Filters do the opposite, filtering out longer wavelengths to improve contrast in bright conditions. Other filters include polarizers and ND filters, each serving unique purposes to manipulate light for creative photography and videography applications.</p></p>
