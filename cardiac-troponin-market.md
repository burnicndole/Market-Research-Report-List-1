<p><p>The cardiac troponin market is categorized into two main types: laboratory testing and point-of-care testing (POCT). Laboratory testing involves centralized, high-throughput analysis in clinical labs, providing detailed results for diagnosing heart conditions. In contrast, point-of-care testing offers rapid results at or near the site of patient care, enhancing immediate decision-making in emergency settings. Both types are essential for the timely detection of myocardial infarctions and other cardiac events, catering to the varying needs of healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
