<p><p>The cataract surgery device market includes various technologies essential for successful procedures. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are artificial lenses implanted to restore vision. Ophthalmic viscoelastic devices (OVDs) provide cushioning and maintain space in the eye during surgery. Phacoemulsification equipment uses ultrasound to break down and remove cataracts, while femtosecond laser equipment enhances precision in cataract removal and lens placement. Together, these devices improve surgical outcomes and patient recovery in cataract surgery.</p></p>
