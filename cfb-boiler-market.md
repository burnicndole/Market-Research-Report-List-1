<p><ul><li>Subcritical CFB Boilers</li><li>Supercritical CFB Boilers</li><li>Ultra Supercritical Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CFB (Circulating Fluidized Bed) boiler market is categorized into three main types: Subcritical, Supercritical, and Ultra Supercritical boilers. Subcritical CFB boilers operate below critical pressure, making them suitable for moderate thermal efficiencies and lower capital costs. Supercritical CFB boilers, operating above critical pressure, enhance efficiency and reduce emissions, making them more environmentally friendly. Ultra Supercritical boilers push efficiency even further with higher pressures and temperatures, representing the advanced technology in CFB systems for reduced fuel costs and improved performance.</p></p>
