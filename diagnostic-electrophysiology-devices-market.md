<p><p>The diagnostic electrophysiology devices market comprises various technologies essential for assessing electrical activity in the body. X-ray systems utilize radiation to visualize internal structures, assisting in diagnosing conditions. Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices measure heart rhythms, crucial for cardiac assessments. Diagnostic electrophysiology (EP) catheters are specialized tools for mapping and treating arrhythmias. Electromyography (EMG) devices evaluate muscle function and nerve conduction, while electroencephalogram (EEG) devices monitor brain waves, aiding in neurological diagnoses. Each device plays a vital role in advancing patient care and outcomes.</p></p>
