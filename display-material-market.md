<p><p>The Display Material Market encompasses several critical components essential for LCD technology. Polarizers enhance image contrast by filtering light waves. Liquid Crystals control light modulation to create images. The Backlighting Unit (BLU) illuminates the display, ensuring visibility in various lighting conditions. Additional materials may include adhesives and protective coatings, contributing to overall performance.</p></p>
