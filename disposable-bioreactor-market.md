<p><p>A disposable bioreactor is a single-use vessel commonly used in biopharmaceutical manufacturing and research for the cultivation of cells or microorganisms. These bioreactors are designed to reduce the risk of cross-contamination, lower operational costs, and enhance flexibility in production processes.
