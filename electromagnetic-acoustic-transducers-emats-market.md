<p><p>Electromagnetic Acoustic Transducers (EMATS) are devices that convert electromagnetic energy into acoustic waves, enabling non-destructive testing in various industries. The market for EMATS is categorized into two main types: Electromagnet Type and Permanent Magnet Type. Electromagnet Type EMATS are powered by an external electric current, allowing for adjustable magnetic fields, while Permanent Magnet Type EMATS utilize fixed magnets to generate acoustic waves, offering advantages in simplicity and reliability. Both types serve critical roles in inspection and monitoring applications.</p></p>
