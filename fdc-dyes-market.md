<p><p>The FD&C dyes market is categorized into three main types: powder dyes, granule dyes, and liquid dyes. Powder dyes offer versatility and are commonly used in food and cosmetic applications due to their easy blending capabilities. Granule dyes are often preferred for bulk formulations, providing controlled application and thermal stability. Liquid dyes, known for their ease of use and uniform distribution, are widely utilized in beverages and edible products. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries, catering to specific formulation needs.</p></p>
