<p><p>The fiberglass sheet market is primarily categorized into flat and corrugated types. Flat fiberglass sheets are smooth, offering a sleek surface ideal for applications in construction, signage, and automotive industries. They provide durability and flexibility while maintaining a lightweight profile. In contrast, corrugated fiberglass sheets are characterized by their ribbed structure, enhancing strength and impact resistance. This type is commonly used in roofing, greenhouse panels, and industrial applications, where superior load-bearing capabilities and weather resistance are essential.</p></p>
