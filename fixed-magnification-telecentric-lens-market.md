<p><p>The Fixed Magnification Telecentric Lens market encompasses various types, including Object Square Telephoto Lenses and Bi-Telecentric Lenses. Object Square Telephoto Lenses offer a uniform magnification across the image field, ideal for high-precision applications like measurement tasks. In contrast, Bi-Telecentric Lenses maintain constant magnification both for object and image space, ensuring that the features' sizes remain consistent, regardless of focus. These lenses are crucial in industrial automation and imaging systems, where accuracy and consistency are paramount.</p></p>
