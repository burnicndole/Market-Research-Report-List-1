<p><strong>The General Electronic Components Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Components</li><li>Passive Components</li><li>Electromechanical Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The general electronic components market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Active Components**: These components can introduce power to a circuit, such as transistors, diodes, and integrated circuits, enabling signal amplification and processing.</p><p>2. **Passive Components**: These do not provide power gain and include resistors, capacitors, and inductors, primarily storing and dissipating energy.</p><p>3. **Electromechanical Components**: Combining electrical and mechanical functions, these components, such as relays and switches, play crucial roles in controlling and switching electrical circuits. Each type contributes uniquely to various electronic applications.</p></p>
