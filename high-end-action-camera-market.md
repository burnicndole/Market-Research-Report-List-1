<p><ul><li>Box Type Camera</li><li>Bullet Type Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end action camera market comprises primarily two types: box type cameras and bullet type cameras. Box type cameras, characterized by their rectangular shape, are designed for versatility, offering features like advanced stabilization, interchangeable lenses, and extensive mounting options, making them suitable for various activities. Bullet type cameras are more compact and streamlined, optimizing portability and ease of use, making them ideal for activities like cycling or motorsports, where minimal wind resistance is key. Both types cater to adventure enthusiasts and content creators.</p></p>
