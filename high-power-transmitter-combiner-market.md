<p><ul><li>Cavity Type</li><li>Hybrid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Power Transmitter Combiner market primarily includes two types: Cavity Type and Hybrid Type. Cavity Type combiners utilize resonant cavities to combine signals, offering high efficiency and low insertion loss, making them ideal for broadcast applications. Hybrid Type combiners, on the other hand, blend different technologies, such as resistive and reactive components, allowing for greater flexibility and compact designs. Both types are essential for maximizing transmission power and ensuring reliable communication across various platforms.</p></p>
