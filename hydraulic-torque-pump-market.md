<p><ul><li>Air Driven</li><li>Electric Driven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic torque pump market is primarily divided into air-driven and electric-driven systems. Air-driven pumps utilize compressed air to create hydraulic pressure, making them suitable for environments where electricity is hazardous. They offer portability and ease of use. In contrast, electric-driven pumps rely on electric motors to generate hydraulic power, providing higher efficiency and consistent performance. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, with air-driven pumps favored in pneumatic settings, while electric-driven pumps are preferred for stationary operations demanding precision and reliability.</p></p>
