<p><p>Hydrophilic gelling fiber dressings are advanced wound care products designed to manage exudate effectively. Composed of water-absorbent fibers, these dressings form a gel-like substance upon contact with wound exudate, providing a moist healing environment that promotes faster tissue regeneration while minimizing pain and discomfort. They are particularly beneficial for chronic and acute wounds, including ulcers, surgical wounds, and burns.</p><p>The hydrophilic gelling fiber dressings market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period.
