Plastic hoses are lightweight, resistant to corrosion, and often used in chemical handling and food processing. Metal hoses provide high durability and heat resistance, commonly used in applications requiring robust performance, such as transfer of steam or high-pressure gases. Each type serves specific industry needs based on material properties.</p></p>
