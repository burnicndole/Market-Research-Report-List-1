<p><p>The LCD driver probe card market is segmented into general pitch and fine pitch categories. General pitch probe cards accommodate larger contact points, making them suitable for testing less complex LCD designs, typically with wider spacing between pads. Fine pitch probe cards, on the other hand, feature closely spaced contacts to test high-density and intricate LCD configurations, providing greater precision. These distinctions cater to different manufacturing requirements, enabling efficient testing and optimization of LCD driver circuits for various applications.</p></p>
