<p><ul><li>Thin Film Inductor</li><li>Wire Wound Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal power inductor market includes two primary types: thin film inductors and wire wound inductors. Thin film inductors are characterized by their compact size and high efficiency, making them ideal for applications requiring low profile solutions and superior performance. In contrast, wire wound inductors utilize coiled wire to achieve higher inductance values, providing robustness for applications demanding higher power handling. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial devices, driven by the demand for compact and efficient power management solutions.</p></p>
