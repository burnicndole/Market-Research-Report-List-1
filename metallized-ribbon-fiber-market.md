<p><ul><li>"Single-Mode Fiber"</li><li>"Multimode Fiber"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallized ribbon fiber market is divided into two main types: single-mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single-mode fiber features a small core diameter, allowing only one mode of light to propagate, making it ideal for long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multimode fiber has a larger core, enabling multiple light modes to transmit simultaneously, which is suitable for shorter distances and higher bandwidth applications. Both types are essential for various telecommunications and data transmission needs.</p></p>
