<p><p>The micro pumps market includes several types, each serving unique applications. Diaphragm micropumps utilize flexible membranes to create suction and displacement, ideal for precise fluid handling. Piston micropumps operate with a reciprocating piston, offering reliable fluid delivery and high-pressure capability. Gear micropumps function by rotating gears to transfer fluids, suitable for low-viscosity liquids. Other types encompass various designs like peristaltic and syringe micropumps, catering to specialized needs in medical, automotive, and industrial sectors, enhancing fluid management and control.</p></p>
