<p><strong>The Optics Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Optical Modules</li><li>Multi Mode Optical Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optics module market encompasses two primary types: single-mode and multi-mode optical modules. Single-mode optical modules are designed for long-range communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize signal loss and interference over extended distances, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, multi-mode optical modules support shorter distances, using multiple light paths to transmit data simultaneously, which enhances bandwidth and efficiency in local area networks and within buildings. Each type caters to distinct networking applications based on distance and performance requirements.</p></p>
