<p><p>The Polyvinyl Acetate (PVAc) dispersion market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based dispersions are environmentally friendly, offering low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and are widely used in adhesives, paints, and coatings. In contrast, solvent-based dispersions contain organic solvents, providing superior performance in terms of adhesion and durability but may pose health and environmental challenges. Both types serve distinct applications, with water-based dispersions gaining popularity due to growing eco-consciousness.</p></p>
