<p><p>Poultry hatchery automation equipment encompasses various technologies designed to optimize incubation processes. Walk-in incubators are large, room-like structures that facilitate the incubation of eggs in bulk, offering precise temperature and humidity controls for better hatch rates. Tunnel-type incubators, on the other hand, utilize a continuous flow system to ensure uniform airflow and even temperature distribution, enhancing embryo development. Both types focus on efficiency, consistency, and improving overall production capacity within poultry hatcheries.</p></p>
