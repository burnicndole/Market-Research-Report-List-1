<p><ul><li>Conventional Toner</li><li>Chemically Prepared Toner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printer toner market is primarily divided into two types: conventional toner and chemically prepared toner. Conventional toner consists of simple, solid particles created through grinding, resulting in a rough texture and varied particle size. In contrast, chemically prepared toner is engineered using a controlled chemical process, producing uniform particle size and shape, which enhances print quality and reduces energy consumption. This differentiation impacts applications across various industries, influencing performance, cost efficiency, and environmental considerations in printing technologies.</p></p>
