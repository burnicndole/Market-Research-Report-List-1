<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Renal Colic Treatment Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL)</li><li>Ureteroscopy (URS)</li><li>Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy (PCNL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The renal colic treatment equipment market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL)** uses sound waves to break kidney stones into smaller pieces, facilitating their passage.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Ureteroscopy (URS)** involves inserting a thin instrument through the urinary tract to directly remove or break up stones.</p><p>3. **Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy (PCNL)** is a minimally invasive surgical procedure for larger stones, accessed through the kidney via a small incision. Each method offers distinct advantages based on the stone's size and location.</p></p>
