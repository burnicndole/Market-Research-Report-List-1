<p><strong>The RFID Handheld Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID Handheld Reader</li><li>HF RFID Handheld Reader</li><li>UHF RFID Handheld Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID handheld reader market is categorized into three main types: LF (Low Frequency) RFID handheld readers operate at 125-134 kHz, ideal for short-range applications like animal tracking. HF (High Frequency) RFID handheld readers function at 13.56 MHz, commonly used for access control and smart cards. UHF (Ultra High Frequency) RFID handheld readers work between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, enabling long-range reading suitable for inventory management and supply chain tracking. Each type serves distinct applications based on frequency and range.</p></p>
