<p><ul><li>Composite Membranes</li><li>Asymmetric Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO membrane market is primarily divided into two types: composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes consist of multiple layers, offering enhanced permeability and selectivity for water while effectively rejecting ions and contaminants. Asymmetric membranes, on the other hand, feature a porous substrate with a thin selective layer, providing structural support and efficient filtration. Both types are essential in various applications, including water treatment, industrial processes, and desalination, catering to the growing demand for clean water and efficient separation technologies.</p></p>
