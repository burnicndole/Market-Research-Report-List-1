<p><p>The silicone hygienic diaphragm valve market features two primary types: weir type and weirless radial diaphragm type. </p><p>Weir type valves utilize a raised seat, promoting effective sealing and flow control, ideal for hygienic applications in the food and pharmaceutical industries. </p><p>In contrast, weirless radial diaphragm valves offer a streamlined design, reducing dead space and enhancing cleanliness by minimizing fluid retention. These valves are increasingly favored in applications demanding rigorous hygiene standards and efficient cleaning processes.</p></p>
