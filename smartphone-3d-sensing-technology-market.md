<p><p>The smartphone 3D sensing technology market encompasses various types, including stereoscopic vision, structured light, time of flight, and ultrasound. Stereoscopic vision uses dual cameras to create depth perception. Structured light projects patterns onto objects to capture their shape. Time of flight measures the time taken for light to reflect from surfaces, providing accurate distance information. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves to gauge distances. The "others" category includes emerging technologies that enhance 3D sensing capabilities, catering to diverse applications in smartphones.</p></p>
