<p><strong>The SOEC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxygen Ion Conducting</li><li>Proton Conducting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid Oxide Electrolyzer Cell (SOEC) market is primarily divided into oxygen ion conducting and proton conducting segments. Oxygen ion conducting SOECs utilize oxygen ions while facilitating high-temperature electrolysis, making them suitable for efficient hydrogen production from steam. In contrast, proton conducting SOECs operate at lower temperatures, leveraging protons for electrolysis, which enables better performance with non-precious materials. Both technologies aim to enhance energy efficiency and support sustainable hydrogen production, catering to the growing demand for clean energy solutions.</p></p>
