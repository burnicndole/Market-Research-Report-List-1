<p><strong>The Spark Plug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Spark Plugs</li><li>Cold Spark Plugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spark plug market is categorized into hot and cold spark plugs, based on their thermal characteristics. Hot spark plugs have a longer insulator nose, retaining heat to prevent fouling in low-performance engines and are suitable for everyday driving. Cold spark plugs feature a shorter insulator, dissipating heat quickly, making them ideal for high-performance and racing applications where increased heat resistance is critical. This differentiation helps optimize engine performance and efficiency under various operating conditions.</p></p>
