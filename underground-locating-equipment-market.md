<p><p>The underground locating equipment market encompasses several key types designed for detecting various subsurface features. Fault detectors identify distortions in ground structures, aiding in infrastructure assessment. Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) provides high-resolution images of subsoil structures, useful in archaeology and construction. Metal detectors locate metallic objects, essential for utility detection and archaeological surveys. Acoustic detectors utilize sound waves to locate underground pipes and cables. Other tools may include electromagnetic devices or visual inspection technologies, offering a range of solutions for underground locating needs.</p></p>
