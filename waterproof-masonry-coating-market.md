<p><p>Waterproof masonry coating comes in two primary types: smooth and rough. The smooth type features a sleek, polished surface that provides an even finish while effectively repelling moisture, making it ideal for aesthetic applications. In contrast, the rough type has a textured finish that enhances adhesion and offers additional protection against water infiltration, suitable for more robust environments. Both types serve to prolong the life of masonry structures by preventing water damage, but they cater to different aesthetic and functional needs.</p></p>
