<p><p>The wooden frame fire windows market encompasses two main types: fixed fire windows and movable fire windows. Fixed fire windows are designed to remain in place during a fire, providing passive fire protection by preventing the spread of flames and smoke. Movable fire windows, on the other hand, can be opened or closed and typically include features that allow for ventilation while maintaining fire safety. Both types are essential for enhancing safety measures in buildings, particularly in areas with strict fire regulations.</p></p>
